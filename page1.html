My Neighbor Totoro

This is my first personal project that I made of the Japanese character Totoro from the movie “My Neighbor Totoro”. I found a picture online and traced it using a pencil onto the piece of fabric and got started on embroidering the pattern onto the fabric. The type of stitching that I used to make Totoro is a mini straight stitch and I was strategic in the colors that I used as I wanted to capture the most accurate design of Totoro. It took me a couple of months to make and was finished in January 2025.
