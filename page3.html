Flowers Project

This is my very first embroidery project that I did while learning embroidery in highschool. My fashion design 2 teacher gave me this assignment to do where we used this flower pattern to practice several different stitches. I used a multitude of colors to make the flowers and stems. The several stitches that I used includes; Straight, Woven-Wheel, Detached-Chain, French Knot, Back Stitch and Stem stitches. This project took me about a month to complete and was the beginning of me learning embroidery.
